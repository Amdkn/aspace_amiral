# BRAINSTORM: Pulse Monitor

## 1. Problem Statement
We need a way to monitor the "pulse" of our business operations locally to ensure stability.

## 2. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
Amiral (Technician) and Agents.

## 3. Angle / Unique Value Proposition
Simple, lightweight, and integrated with the A'Space OS PARA structure.

## 4. Risks & Constraints
Must not consume excessive resources. Must be readable by other agents.
